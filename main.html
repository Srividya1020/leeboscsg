Welcome to Leebo’s Sickle Cell Support Group

We are dedicated to supporting individuals and families affected by sickle cell disease. Our mission is to help those in need through resources, care, and a community that understands the challenges they face.

What We Provide

Counseling and Support

We provide emotional and mental health support to individuals and families, helping them cope with challenges.

Education and Outreach

We raise awareness and educate the community about sickle cell disease through workshops and events.

Medical Resources

We connect individuals with the medical resources they need to manage their sickle cell disease effectively.

Leebo's Legacy: Our Story

Leebo’s journey began with a vision to support those living with sickle cell disease. Over the years, we have grown into a community that provides emotional, medical, and educational resources to individuals and families affected by the disease. With our collective effort, we continue to honor Leebo's legacy and impact countless lives.

From counseling and community outreach to medical support and resources, our group stands as a pillar of hope and strength for all those navigating the challenges of sickle cell disease. Our story is one of resilience, faith, and unwavering support.

Meet Our Ambassador

Danasia Monea Beckett Watts

Her Story

“My first crisis occurred around my first birthday. By age 10, I had endured numerous surgeries, including gallbladder removal and mediport placements. Despite frequent hospitalizations, I pursued my dreams and strived for a normal life. At 18, I faced severe complications, leading to a medically induced coma and multiple joint replacements. Through my family’s support and my faith, I have persevered.”

Her Message

“To my fellow warriors, you are not alone; you have a family in 'Leebo’s Sickle Cell Support Group.'”
